Who is Shikorina Zurya

Welcome to "Shikorina Zurya " Online Shop, your number one source for all Eritrean Traditional. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of the Habesha cultural clothes & accessories, with an emphasis on cultural, originality & sophistication. Founded in 2022 by Soliana Habte, "Shikorina Zurya " has come a long way from its beginnings in Asmara. When we first started, our passion for creating divine art through fashion drove us to start our own business. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
